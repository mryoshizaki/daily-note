Notes Guide



In addition to tracking events and tasks, Daily Note can keep track of notes for its users to remember.

Creating a Note:

1. On the Navigation Bar on the top of the website, click the Notes section.

2. On the top right hand corner, click the Add New Note button.

3. On the modal window that appears, enter the title of the note and its contents.

4. Click the Save button to save the note, or the Cancel button to abort.

Editing/Updating a Note

1. On the Navigation Bar on the top of the website, click the Notes section.

2. Click the Update button on top of the note you wish to edit.

3. Replace the title and/or contents of the note.

4. Click the Update button to save your changes, and the Cancel button to abort.

Deleting a Note:

1. On the Navigation Bar on the top of the website, click the Notes section.

2. Click the Delete button on top of the note you wish to delete.
