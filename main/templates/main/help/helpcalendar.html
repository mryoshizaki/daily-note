Calendar Guide



Daily Note provides also provides its users with a calendar view of the events and tasks that need to be accomplished at hand. Through the calendar view, users can add events/tasks that they need to take note of. Users can select if they want a monthly view, weekly view, and daily view of the calendar. A Today button is also present for the users to quickly go back to the current date on the calendar.

Adding an Event/Task

1. On the Navigation Bar on the top of the website, click the Calendar section.

2. Click on the date you wish to assign an event/task on.

3. On the top right hand corner, click the Add Event button.

4. On the modal window that appears, choose whether a task or an event will be taken note of.

5. Enter the event title, description.

6. Enter the start date and end date by clicking the interactive calendar on the side.

7. Click the Save button to save the event/task, or the Close button to abort.

Updating an Event/Task

1. On the Navigation Bar on the top of the website, click the Calendar section.

Deleting an Event/Task

1. On the Navigation Bar on the top of the website, click the Calendar section.
