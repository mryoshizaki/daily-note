Dashboard Guide



Daily Note provides its users with a dashboard that would help its users in keeping track of the events or tasks that they need to accomplish. The dashboard will display what event/s or task/s are currently being accomplished, as well as the date and time of their completion. Items in the dashboard are taken from the events and tasks listed under your calendar. Through this view, users can update and deletes existing events/tasks in the list.

Updating an Event/Task

1. On the table of events / to do list, click the update action button to edit the details of your chosen event/task.

Deleting an Event/Task

1. On the table of events / to do list, click the delete action button to remove your chosen event/task from the list.
